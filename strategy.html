Innocent Strategies:

The Detective's Shadow

Stick together with the detective in large numbers. Walking in a pack makes it hard for the Traitors to pick anyone off, but also easier for them to take everyone out with an explosive.

The Vibe Checker

Pay attention to who is not talking or is going off into secluded rooms.

Traitor Strategies:

The Third Party Killer

Wait for two Innocents to start arguing or shoot each other, then finish one off. They'll blame each other!

The RDMer

Though I don't recommend this one. There are ways to play it off. In general, rdming will just you killed. RDM is when you kill anyone the moment you see them.

Detective Strategies:

The Sheriff

Attempt to call a town meeting and bring everyone together. If the Traitors are skilled, they will cause some great chaos.

The President

Get two people to follow you. It is unlikely they will both be Traitors. If one of them is a Traitor, they'll have to make the choice if they really want to trade their life for the Detective's life (the other player would be Innocent and shoot them). If both are Innocents, then you've got a team!
